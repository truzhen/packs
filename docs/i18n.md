## 繁體中文

# 徒真（truzhen）Packs

**讓你的開源能力和行業經驗，被真實企業買來用。**

[三類 Pack](pack-types.md) · [作者指南](author-guide.md) · [如何提交](submit-pack.md) · [收益與市場](revenue-and-market.md) · [審核與上架](review-and-listing.md)

徒真（truzhen）Packs 面向開源作者、工具作者和行業高手。你可以把 CLI、SDK、MCP Server、自動化工具、服務方法或行業經驗，做成企業可以安裝、試用、購買的工作包。

企業買到的不是一段 prompt，也不是一個無邊界插件。Pack 只負責提出能力、草稿、流程和風險提示；發送、執行、寫入正式資料等關鍵動作，必須回到企業負責人確認和徒真受控鏈路，並留下可回放的記錄。

### 三類 Pack

| 你是誰 | 你做的 Pack | 企業買到的是 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 開源軟體作者 | 能力包 | 可被受控調用的專業能力，例如 PDF 解析、資料整理、內容生成或專案快照讀取。 |
| 行業高手 | 場景包 | 可直接開工的行業工作台，包含流程、角色、證據要求和協作方法。 |
| 角色設計者 | 角色包 | 具有特定風格與判斷框架的 AI 角色，用來提建議、挑問題和打草稿。 |

### 作者能得到什麼

- 你的能力進入不逛 GitHub 的真實企業場景。
- Pack 通過審核與試裝後，可以在市場中被購買、訂閱或買斷，作者按平台規則獲得收益；不承諾固定收入。
- 你不需要自己重做權限、審計、支付、分發和企業交付邊界。

### 第一次交什麼最合適

不要從大而全開始。先交一件企業今天就願意付錢解決的小事：抽取一批 PDF 的關鍵資訊、幫客服主管複核話術、把家政預約從諮詢到回訪串起來。第一版要說清楚服務誰、解決哪件事、需要哪些外部能力、哪些動作必須企業負責人確認。

## English

# truzhen Packs

**Turn your open-source capability and domain know-how into work packages that real businesses can buy and use.**

[Pack Types](pack-types.md) · [Author Guide](author-guide.md) · [Submit a Pack](submit-pack.md) · [Revenue and Market](revenue-and-market.md) · [Review and Listing](review-and-listing.md)

truzhen Packs are for open-source maintainers, tool builders, and domain experts. A Pack turns a CLI, SDK, MCP Server, automation tool, service method, or industry workflow into a work package that a business can install, try, and buy.

Businesses are not buying an unbounded plugin or a prompt bundle. A Pack proposes capabilities, drafts, workflows, and risk notes. High-impact actions such as sending, executing, or writing formal data must go back to the business owner for confirmation, pass through truzhen's controlled path, and leave replayable records.

### Three Pack Types

| Who you are | Pack type | What a business gets |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Open-source maintainer | Capability Pack | A controlled professional capability such as PDF parsing, customer data cleanup, content generation, or project snapshot reading. |
| Domain expert | Scene Pack | A ready-to-run industry workspace with flows, roles, evidence requirements, and working methods. |
| Role designer | Role Pack | An AI role with a specific tone, judgment style, and review habit for advice, critique, and drafting. |

### What authors can gain

- Your capability reaches real small-business workflows, not only README visitors.
- After review and trial installation, a Pack can be listed for purchase, subscription, or buyout under platform rules. Revenue is possible but not guaranteed.
- You do not need to rebuild permissions, audit logs, payment, distribution, and enterprise delivery boundaries yourself.

### Best first submission

Do not start with a grand all-in-one system. Start with one concrete job a business would pay to solve today: extract key fields from PDFs, review customer-service scripts, or connect consultation, scheduling, and follow-up for a local service team. The first version should state who it serves, what job it solves, what external capabilities it needs, and which actions require owner confirmation.
